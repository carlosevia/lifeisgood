How do I use Google Gemini?

Google Gemini, like ChatGPT, accepts input questions from you and generates full-text responses.

Use this procedure to access Google Gemini and to enter a query.

  1. In your web browser, open https://gemini.google.com/.
  2. Enter the following question.
    What can Gemini do for me?
  3. Review Gemini's response.
